## Overview

**Michelangelo: Vibe Coding** streamlines the development of **native Expo apps** by combining advanced **AI**, automatic code generation, and an optimized mobile interface. 
With a **native iOS user experience**, full support for **Expo** and **React Native**, fast distribution for **iOS**, and automatic synchronization with **GitHub**, the app provides developers with a **powerful** and **efficient environment** to create and deliver **high-quality native applications** directly from your iPhone, wherever you are.
