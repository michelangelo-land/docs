Imaginer is the annual supporter subscription for Michelangelo. By becoming an Imaginer, you unlock the full app experience and help keep the project independent.

## Why Become an Imaginer

Michelangelo is built without outside investors. Every subscription directly supports development, infrastructure, and new features.

As an Imaginer, you also get:

- **An ad-free Explore feed** — focus on discovering apps without interruptions.
- **Full access to generation features** — create and iterate without restrictions.
- **The satisfaction of supporting independent software** — you keep the project moving forward.

## Choose Your Support Level

The subscription screen lets you pick an annual support level:

| Tier | Price |
| ---- | ----- |
| Subs10 | $10 / year |
| Subs15 | $15 / year |
| Subs20 | $20 / year |

All tiers unlock the same features.

## How to Subscribe

1. Open the subscription screen from the app.
2. Slide to choose your support level.
3. Tap **Become an Imaginer**.
4. Complete the purchase through the App Store.

<Info>
  Subscriptions renew automatically every year. You can cancel or restore your subscription at any time from your App Store account.
</Info>

## Restore Purchases

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, tap **Restore Purchases** on the subscription screen. Your active subscription is tied to your Apple ID.
