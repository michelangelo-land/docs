Michelangelo Web extends the Michelangelo creation experience to desktop browsers, giving you a larger workspace and a keyboard-first workflow.

## Why Web?

While the iPhone is perfect for quick creation on the go, the web is ideal for longer sessions. You can write longer prompts, manage multiple projects side by side, and share previews directly from your browser.

## What You Will Be Able to Do

- Create projects from detailed prompts.
- Review and interact with live previews.
- Iterate with follow-up messages.
- Sync projects across mobile and web.
- Share public preview links.
- Upload reference images from your computer.

## Consistent With Mobile

The web experience shares the same design language, project structure, and AI behavior as the iOS app. A project created on mobile will look and behave the same when opened on the web.

<Info>
  Michelangelo Web is currently in development and will enter early access in the future.
</Info>
