---
title: "Share Preview"
description: "Share your app with a simple public link."
---

Want to show your app before the official deployment? Michelangelo.land lets you **share a live preview of your project** with a simple public link. <br />
1. Click on the ‘<Icon icon="arrow-up-from-bracket" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" /> **Publish**‘ button in the top-right corner of interface and a popover will open with publish options.
2. Locate the **Share Preview** section inside the popover.
3. Toggle the **switch** to enable or disable the preview link:
    - <Icon icon="eye" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" /> **enable** will make the link active and **visible to the public**.
    - <Icon icon="eye-slash" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" /> **disable** will make the link inactive and **not visible to the public**.
4. Click the <Icon icon="copy" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" /> clipboard icon to **copy the link**.
5. **Share the link** with anyone — no login or Michelangelo account required to access the preview.
