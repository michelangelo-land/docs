In the '**Profile**' section, you can manage all your account information and settings on Michelangelo. The page is divided into several areas:

### <Icon icon="user" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" /> Basic Information
Here you can view your personal details, including:
- **Name**
- **Email**
- **Profile picture**

### <Icon icon="credit-card" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" />  Account
This area displays your **current subscription plan** (e.g. "Pro") and allows you to manage your subscription using the '**Manage subscription**' button.

### <Icon icon="chart-line" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" />  Usage
Check your **remaining token balance** for your account. If you need help, you can contact support [here](https://docs.michelangelo.land/introduction#support).

### <Icon icon="handshake" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" />  Thanks, Contributor!
If you [contribute to Michelangelo](https://michelangelo.land/contributors), this section displays your current earnings and remaining balance. Here you can keep track of any rewards or payments you have received for your contributions.

### <Icon icon="credit-card" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" />  Spending Limit (Beta)
This section is only visible if you are subscribed to the **Pay-as-You-Go** plan. It allows **you to set a spending limit** for your account. You can adjust the limit according to your needs using the plus and minus buttons. Note that the limit cannot be set below your current balance. This feature helps you control and manage your expenses on the platform.

### <Icon icon="key" iconType="regular" color="#7c7c7c" size="20" />  API (Beta)
In this section, you can **request and manage your API key** (if available). You will also find a link to the official [API documentation](/api-call/your-api-call).

<br />

Each section is designed to give you quick access to essential information and controls for your Michelangelo.land account.
